About Better Questions

Better Questions is a free tool designed to help patients surface medically-supported questions to ask their doctors— using trusted data from PubMed, not speculation.

It does not attempt to diagnose. Instead, it connects symptoms and known conditions to peer-reviewed research, and frames questions you can take into your next appointment.

Why Might I Get No Results?

This tool is designed to avoid hallucinations. If no questions appear, it means no documented matches were found for all the symptoms and conditions you entered. Try entering fewer items or searching in smaller chunks.

In future versions, support for partial matches will be added—with clear labeling to protect accuracy.

Ethics & Privacy

Better Questions collects no personal data. It runs without user tracking, ads, or logins. You can even use it in private/incognito mode. It’s built for patients—not profit.
